## Key Contributions

- Shared how Notebook LM's retrieval architecture was progressively simplified as Gemini's context window grew from small to 1 million tokens, reducing the need for chunking infrastructure
- Revealed that the 270M parameter Gemma model had more demand than expected because people valued putting it where large models cannot go (phones, Raspberry Pi, persistent local processes), leading to Function Gemma for local tool calling
- Articulated the timeless product advice: "solve people's problems with whatever tools exist" -- the levels of abstraction stack up faster than ever, but the core remains the same
- Discussed the Pareto frontier of intelligence vs. latency/cost from Waymo's millisecond decisions to deep research that can run for hours
