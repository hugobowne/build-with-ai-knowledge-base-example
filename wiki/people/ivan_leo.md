## Key Contributions

- Built a ~1,500-line deep research agent from scratch demonstrating sub-agent architecture with dynamic spawning, iteration limits, and context compartmentalization
- Articulated the "spend today for the models of tomorrow" principle: build for capabilities that will become cheaper, not just what is cheap now
- Introduced meta-prompting (asking models to improve their own tool descriptions and prompts) as a practical technique enabled by improved model capabilities
- Created Kura, an open-source tool for clustering and observability of agentic traces
