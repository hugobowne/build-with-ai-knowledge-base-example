## Key Contributions

- Created the "proof-of-concept purgatory" framework and five first principles of AI software development (API calls, non-determinism, logging, evaluation, iteration) that structure the entire course
- Authored "Stop Building AI Agents" (hit #1 on Hacker News), then publicly evolved his position to distinguish AI autonomy from AI autonomy without supervision
- Championed the reframing of coding agents as general-purpose computer-using agents, demonstrated through building agents from scratch in multiple sessions
- Established context engineering as distinct from prompt engineering: "building the machine that populates the prompt"
