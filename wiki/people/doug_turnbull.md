## Key Contributions

- Co-authored "Relevant Search," the industry standard book for search engineering
- Created the search array library (pandas/numpy-based BM25 implementation) for rapid in-memory search prototyping
- Delivered the most accessible NDCG explanation in the course: "adding up weighted relevance labels where top positions get more weight, then normalizing by ideal ordering" -- "really big words for grade school math"
- Articulated the precision vs. recall tradeoff across domains: legal discovery demands recall (catch everything), consumer e-commerce demands precision (top results must be right)
